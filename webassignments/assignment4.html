Challenge 5 - Accessibility Statement

As with everything in our modern world, accessibility is always something that we should strive towards in creating websites. In this particular assignment, there were a few areas that could be improved upon to make them more accessible. For example, the chessboard exercise is complete, but the pieces could be made more distinct from one another for people who are visually impaired. One could say the same about the coloring of the Google search page. The dark purple with the black background is not the easiest thing to read. Overall, these challenges could definitely be improved.
